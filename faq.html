Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the 'Create Account' button on the top right corner of the homepage. Fill in your personal details and click 'Submit'. You will receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

How do I enroll in a course?

To enroll in a course, go to the 'Courses' page, select the course you are interested in, and click the 'Enroll' button. Follow the instructions to complete your enrollment.

For a beginner, which course should they take?

If you are a beginner, we recommend starting with our 'Introduction to Programming' course. This course covers the basics of programming and is a great foundation for more advanced topics.

How can I access the quizzes?

You can access the quizzes by navigating to the 'Quiz' page from the top menu. There, you will find various quizzes to test your knowledge on different subjects.

Can I interact with other students?

Yes, you can interact with other students in the 'Community' section. You can participate in forums, join study groups, and share resources.
